**Domain-specific sections from autoresearch that have NO 1-to-1 task equivalent** and are intentionally omitted from the template:

- `LFM2 SKIPPED` (FX-specific foundation-model decision) — replaced by `framework/sota_catalog.yaml` backbone selection per task type.
- `Super-Fold Invariants` (FX 7-regime hole-punching) — replaced by generic split invariants in section 6.
- `Trade-Level Win/Loss Logging` (FX trading P&L) — re-named to `Per-Sample Decision Logging` for generic decision logging.
- `Heteroscedastic Loss for trading signals` — retained for regression tasks; classification tasks use temperature calibration in its place.

These 4 substitutions are tracked here for transparency; the validator counts the **task-side** section as covering the **autoresearch-side** rule.

## Validator behaviour

`framework/validator.py` reads `SECTION_MAPPING.md`, grabs the "Task CLAUDE.md location" column, and asserts each substring appears in the generated `CLAUDE.md`. Missing rows are written to `<repo>/audit_report_third_party.md` and the repo is marked `not_ready`. The hill-climb loop refuses to launch on a `not_ready` repo.
